    07. Reflections & Key Learnings

    Personal insights and takeaways from the educational tour experience

    Academic Insights

    This educational tour greatly enriched my academic understanding of the IT and engineering fields. Visiting companies such as Alliance Software, Dynata, Rivant IT, Mata Technologies, and Tecdia allowed me to see how the concepts we study in class—systems development, data management, software testing, networking, and hardware engineering—are applied in real industry settings. I observed several industry best practices, including agile workflows, strict version control, proper documentation, and the importance of cybersecurity in everyday operations. Many companies also introduced us to new technologies and tools such as immersive tech systems, automated testing platforms, data analytics dashboards, and modern hardware fabrication techniques. Seeing these firsthand helped me connect classroom theories to real-world applications, making the lessons feel more practical, modern, and impactful. It reinforced the idea that IT is not just about coding—it’s about solving problems, improving processes, and contributing to innovation.

    • Industry best practices observed
    • New technologies and tools discovered
    • Real-world application of classroom learning

    Personal Growth

    Beyond the academic lessons, the tour played a major role in my personal development. Throughout the activities, I became more aware of how I applied CROSSIAN values such as Competence, Respect, Responsibility, Service, and Spirituality. Cultural appreciation and awareness grew as we visited historical and natural sites in Bohol, reminding me of the importance of heritage in shaping innovation. I practiced professional conduct and etiquette during company tours—dressing appropriately, asking meaningful questions, and interacting respectfully with industry representatives. The trip also strengthened my teamwork and collaboration skills. Whether during group activities, shared meals, or navigating schedules, I learned how important communication, cooperation, and patience are when working with others. This tour helped me grow not only as a student but also as a more mature and responsible individual.

    • Cultural appreciation and awareness
    • Professional conduct and etiquette
    • Teamwork and collaboration skills

    Career Perspectives

    This experience significantly influenced how I view my future career. The different companies we visited exposed me to various IT fields—software development, data analytics, hardware engineering, immersive tech, and customer support operations. It gave me clarity on potential career paths, especially the areas that sparked my interest the most. Interacting with professionals helped me understand industry expectations and requirements, such as the need for strong technical foundations, continuous learning, adaptability, and solid soft skills. I also realized the value of building professional networks, even at an early stage, as these connections may help guide future opportunities. The tour strengthened my commitment to pursue an IT career that aligns with both my skills and my passion for innovation.

    • Career path insights and clarity
    • Industry expectations and requirements
    • Professional networking opportunities

    Memorable Moments

    The tour was filled with experiences that left lasting impressions. Among all the activities, a few stood out the most: My favorite cultural site was the Chocolate Hills—its natural beauty felt surreal and reminded me of the wonders found in our own country. The most impactful company visit was Mata Technologies, where we saw innovative immersive technologies that opened my eyes to the future possibilities in tech. Some of the most meaningful moments came from simply spending time with classmates—sharing meals, traveling together, and experiencing new places as a group. These moments strengthened friendships and created memories that I will carry long after the tour has ended. Overall, this educational tour was not just a requirement—it was a transformative journey filled with learning, culture, inspiration, and personal growth.

    • Favorite cultural or historical site
    • Most impactful company visit
    • Special moments with classmates

    Final Thoughts

    This educational tour has been one of the most meaningful experiences of my academic journey. It bridged the gap between classroom learning and the real world, giving me a clearer picture of what it truly means to pursue a career in the IT field. Seeing actual workplaces, meeting industry professionals, and observing real processes gave me confidence and motivation to continue improving my skills. My main takeaway is that success in this industry requires not only technical knowledge, but also adaptability, strong communication, professionalism, and a willingness to learn continuously. The cultural and historical visits in Bohol added a deeper dimension to the trip, reminding me that technology and innovation should always serve people, communities, and heritage. This balance between learning, exploration, and reflection helped me grow both as a student and as an individual preparing for a future career. I am sincerely grateful to our organizers, faculty members, coordinators, and school administration for making this tour possible. Their dedication ensured that we learned, enjoyed, and stayed safe throughout the entire journey. I also want to thank my classmates and fellow students for the camaraderie, cooperation, and unforgettable moments we shared. Together, we turned this tour into a meaningful adventure filled with knowledge, laughter, and lasting memories. This experience has strengthened my resolve to pursue excellence, stay curious, and work hard toward the IT career I aspire to build.
